About Oriflame

A legacy of natural beauty, rooted in Sweden, flourishing across the globe

Oriflame is a pioneering Natural Swedish Skincare and Cosmetics company that has been redefining the beauty industry for over five decades. Founded in 1967 by the brothers Jonas af Jochnick and Robert af Jochnick in Stockholm, Sweden, the brand was born out of a simple yet revolutionary idea — to make high-quality, nature-inspired beauty products accessible to everyone, everywhere. What began as a small Swedish enterprise has since blossomed into one of the world's most recognised direct-selling beauty companies, with a vibrant and ever-growing presence in 64 countries across Europe, Asia, Africa, and Latin America. Today, Oriflame stands as a beacon of Scandinavian beauty philosophy, combining the purity of Nordic nature with cutting-edge scientific research to deliver products that truly make a difference.

At the heart of Oriflame lies an unwavering commitment to nature. Every product is thoughtfully formulated using naturally derived ingredients, sourced responsibly from some of the most pristine environments on the planet. From the lush botanical gardens of Sweden to the mineral-rich waters of the Nordic fjords, Oriflame harnesses the very best that nature has to offer. The company's world-class research and development facilities employ over 100 scientists who work tirelessly to combine these natural ingredients with advanced biotechnology, ensuring that every cream, serum, lipstick, and fragrance delivers visible, long-lasting results. Oriflame proudly maintains a strict policy against animal testing and is committed to sustainable sourcing, eco-friendly packaging, and reducing its environmental footprint — because true beauty should never come at the cost of our planet.

The Oriflame product portfolio is nothing short of extraordinary. Spanning across skincare, makeup, fragrance, personal care, hair care, and wellness, the brand offers over 1,000 innovative products that cater to every age, skin type, and lifestyle. Whether you are looking for an advanced anti-ageing serum from the iconic NovAge range, a show-stopping lipstick from The ONE collection, a refreshing fragrance that captures the essence of Scandinavian nature, or a wellness supplement to nourish your body from within, Oriflame has something for everyone. Each product is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, blending luxurious textures, captivating scents, and powerful active ingredients to create an experience that goes far beyond ordinary beauty.

What truly sets Oriflame apart is its unique business model and the incredible community it has built over the years. With a global network of over 3 million Brand Partners, Oriflame offers individuals from all walks of life the opportunity to start their own beauty business with zero investment. Whether you are a student, a stay-at-home parent, a working professional, or a retiree, you can join Oriflame and earn generous commissions while sharing products that you genuinely love. The company provides comprehensive training, marketing support, digital tools, and mentorship programmes to help every Brand Partner succeed, regardless of their background or experience level.

Browsing through our catalogues is not just shopping — it is a sensory experience that transports you to the heart of Swedish beauty and ignites your passion for self-care and glamour.

Beyond products and business, Oriflame is deeply committed to making a positive impact on society. Through the Oriflame Foundation, the company supports numerous charitable initiatives around the world, focusing on children's education, women's empowerment, and environmental conservation. Every purchase you make contributes to these noble causes, making your beauty routine a force for good. Oriflame believes that beauty is not just about how you look — it is about how you feel, how you inspire others, and how you leave the world a little better than you found it.
